Delete Your Account

How to request deletion of your My Nail Connection account and data · Last updated: April 28, 2026

This page explains how to request that your My Nail Connection account, and the personal data we hold about you, be deleted. It applies to both clients and nail technicians using the Service.

Request deletion in one step

Email us from the email address on your account, with the subject line "Delete my account". That's all we need.

Email anne@mynailconnection.com

What happens after you email us

  1. We confirm it's you. We'll reply to verify the request came from the email registered on your account. (This protects you from someone else asking to delete your account.)
  2. We delete your account and personal data within 30 days. See below for the full list of what gets removed.
  3. We email you a confirmation when the deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

✓ Removed within 30 days

  • Your account record (email, name, password hash)
  • Your profile content — biography, profile photo, contact preferences
  • For techs: portfolio photos, service tags, location, shop info, hours, booking link, and any other profile fields
  • Saved/favorited techs and search history
  • Push notification subscriptions for your devices
  • Any messages, feedback, or in-app communications you sent
  • Account preferences and settings

What we keep, and for how long

A small amount of information has to be retained for legal and operational reasons even after your account is deleted. We keep only what we're required to keep, and only for as long as we have to.

Kept for legal/financial compliance

  • Payment records (techs only): retained as required by U.S. tax and accounting law — typically 7 years. We keep transaction records (date, amount, Stripe transaction ID) but no card details. We never had your card number to begin with — Stripe handles those directly.
  • Security and abuse logs: retained for up to 12 months to investigate fraud, harassment, or terms-of-service violations. Includes IP addresses associated with sign-in events.

Aggregate analytics (no longer linked to you)

  • De-identified, aggregate usage statistics (e.g. "X users signed up in March") may be retained indefinitely. None of this is linked back to your identity once your account is deleted.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install the app to delete my account?

No. You can email us at any time, whether or not you still have the app installed.

Can I export my data before deleting?

Yes. Email us with the subject line "Export my data" before requesting deletion, and we'll send you a copy of your account info and content within 30 days.

What if I want to delete just some data, not my whole account?

Email us and let us know what you'd like removed (e.g. specific photos, your phone number). We can usually make targeted edits without closing the account.

Can I undo a deletion?

Once we've completed deletion, we can't recover the data. If you change your mind within the 30-day window before deletion is processed, email us and we'll cancel the request.

How do I know my data was actually deleted?

You'll receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete. After that, attempting to log in with your old email will return "no account found."

Questions or concerns?

Email anne@mynailconnection.com at any time. We aim to reply to deletion requests within two business days.
