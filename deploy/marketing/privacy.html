Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Pre-launch placeholder. Our full privacy policy is being finalized with counsel ahead of launch. The summary below reflects our intent. If you have questions or want to request deletion before the formal policy publishes, email hello@mynailconnection.com.

What we collect

Account info you give us (name, email, phone if you add one to your tech profile), portfolio photos you upload, and basic analytics (pages viewed, approximate location from IP, device type) to understand how the app is used.

How we use it

To run the app: match clients to techs, show your profile in search and on the map, send transactional emails (signup confirmation, receipts, password resets). We don't sell your data, and we don't send marketing email without your say-so.

Who we share it with

Our infrastructure providers (Supabase for accounts & storage, Stripe for payments, email delivery providers for transactional mail). Each processes data on our behalf under their own privacy commitments.

Your rights

You can ask us to export or delete your account and data at any time. Email hello@mynailconnection.com from the address on the account and we'll handle it within 30 days. EU / UK / California residents have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA — the same email address gets you to the right place.

Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies required to keep you signed in, and a privacy-respecting analytics tool to measure pageviews in aggregate. No third-party ad tracking.
