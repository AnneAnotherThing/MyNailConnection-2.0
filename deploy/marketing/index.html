01

Build a Profile That Gets You Found

Fill in your shop name, full address, bio, and a profile photo. Clients use the map to find techs near them — no address means invisible on the map. A face or brand photo makes clients three times more likely to reach out. Mobile tech? Tag yourself Mobile and your street address is kept private automatically — clients see your service area, not your home base.

02

Tag Every Photo — Critical

When clients filter by shape, type, or style, only tagged photos appear. An untagged photo is invisible to filtered searches — which is how most clients browse. Tag each photo with Shape, Material, and Style so the right clients find your work. The Tag My Photos dashboard makes catching up on older photos fast — untagged ones float to the top, and your changes auto-save as you tap.

03

Set Your Availability — Get Seen First

Toggle Available Right Now or Available This Week from your dashboard. Clients searching for same-day appointments will see you first. This is completely free and takes two seconds.

04

Connect Your Way — Call, Text, or Book

MNC doesn't replace how clients reach you — it gets more of them there. Add your phone number and clients call or text directly from your profile. On Vagaro, GlossGenius, StyleSeat, or Square? Add your booking link and a Book Now button appears automatically. Each phone number is tied to a single MNC account, so your listing stays uniquely yours.

05

Post Updates. Stay Top of Mind.

Share availability windows, promos, and nail inspo using the New Post button. Posts appear on your public profile — another free way to stay visible with clients who already love your work. The board is intentionally one-way: no comments, no replies, no DM threads. Clients read the update and reach out on your terms — a call, a text, or a tap on your booking link.

06

A Weekly Rhythm — Glow Up

When clients browse by style, photos appear in a smart rotation. Every tech gets seen — but techs with more photos appear more often. Start with 5 free slots (your highlight reel), then unlock Glow Up for 5 new photo slots every week. Fresh slots land every Sunday — no thinking, no catching up. Post this week's sets, watch your portfolio compound over time, and let clients find the range of your work.
